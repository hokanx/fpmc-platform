### 4.1 · LOGO STING (2–3s, intro/outro for ALL videos) — produce first

**SHOT-BY-SHOT EFFECTS TIMELINE**

SHOT 1 (00:00–00:01) — Emergence
• EFFECT: Volumetric light reveal + particle condensation
• Pure void-black frame (#0A0A0A). A single horizontal beam of cold white light sweeps left-to-right across the darkness, like a film projector beam cutting through a dark cinema
• Fine dust particles ignite inside the beam as it passes, drifting slowly
• Camera static, locked-off — the light does the moving
• The beam pass leaves a faint after-glow where the logo will appear

SHOT 2 (00:01–00:02.5) — Logo Materialization — SIGNATURE VISUAL EFFECT
• EFFECT: Light-trace assembly + subtle scale settle
• The FPMC logo assembles from thin traces of light following its contours, as if drawn by the projector beam — strokes complete within ~0.8s
• Once complete, the logo settles with a barely perceptible scale-down (approx. 102% → 100%) — a breath, not a bounce
• Faint film-grain texture over the whole frame, monochrome
• A soft vignette holds the edges in deep black

SHOT 3 (00:02.5–00:03) — Hold & Resolve
• EFFECT: Slow luminance decay
• The logo holds, its glow dims approx. 15% to a steady, confident brightness
• Particles fade out completely; frame is clean logo on void black
• Ends on a stable frame suitable as freeze/cut point

**MASTER EFFECTS INVENTORY**
1. Volumetric light sweep (1x, Shot 1) — the projector-beam signature, ties to the "Lichtspiel" brand idea
2. Particle condensation (1x, Shot 1) — atmosphere without clutter
3. Light-trace assembly (1x, Shot 2) — the hero effect, logo drawn by light
4. Scale settle 102→100% (1x, Shot 2) — organic landing
5. Film grain, monochrome (throughout) — cinema texture
6. Luminance decay (1x, Shot 3) — clean resolve

**EFFECTS DENSITY MAP**
00:00–00:01 = MEDIUM (light sweep + particles — 2 effects in 1s)
00:01–00:02.5 = HIGH (trace assembly + settle + grain + vignette — 4 effects in 1.5s)
00:02.5–00:03 = LOW (luminance decay only — clean resolve)

**ENERGY ARC**
Act 1: Darkness broken by a single beam — attention through minimalism, not noise.
Act 2: The light itself builds the brand — the signature moment.
Act 3: Stillness. The logo simply *is*. Quiet confidence as the landing.

*Outro variant: identical prompt, but describe the shot order inverted (logo stands → light dissolves it → beam exit). Sound: Jasper builds a 2s audio sting (deep sub impact + vinyl crackle).*

### 4.2 · WEBSITE HERO LOOP (8–10s, seamless loop, 16:9 + 9:16 reframe)

**SHOT-BY-SHOT EFFECTS TIMELINE**

SHOT 1 (00:00–00:04) — The Beam Room
• EFFECT: Slow parallax drift + volumetric haze
• Wide frame: a dark, empty studio space rendered almost entirely in blacks and graphites — silhouettes of a film light on a stand and a studio microphone barely readable in the darkness
• A single projector beam crosses the frame diagonally, thin haze drifting through it in slow motion (approx. 40% speed)
• Camera drifts laterally right approx. 5% of frame width over 4 seconds — barely perceptible parallax between foreground silhouette and beam
• Dust motes catch light only inside the beam

SHOT 2 (00:04–00:07) — Logo in the Light
• EFFECT: Rack-focus reveal + glow bloom (subtle)
• Focus racks from the drifting haze to the FPMC logo, revealed floating in the beam's path as if projected mid-air
• A restrained bloom on the logo's brightest edges — never blown out
• Camera drift continues at the same speed and direction (continuity for the loop)

SHOT 3 (00:07–00:10) — Return to Haze — LOOP POINT
• EFFECT: Reverse rack-focus + luminance match
• Focus eases back off the logo into the haze; logo softens into the beam
• Frame's final composition, luminance and camera position match SHOT 1's opening exactly — seamless loop
• No cuts anywhere: one continuous take feeling

**MASTER EFFECTS INVENTORY**
1. Lateral parallax drift (throughout) — hypnotic slow movement for a hero background
2. Volumetric haze + dust (throughout) — the Lichtspiel signature
3. Rack focus (2x, Shots 2+3) — reveals and conceals without cutting
4. Subtle glow bloom (1x, Shot 2) — logo presence without kitsch
5. Monochrome grade + film grain (throughout) — achromatic per design system

**EFFECTS DENSITY MAP**
00:00–00:04 = LOW (drift + haze — meditative)
00:04–00:07 = MEDIUM (rack focus + bloom — the reveal)
00:07–00:10 = LOW (reverse focus — resolve into loop)

**ENERGY ARC**
One breath: darkness → appearance → darkness. As a hero background it must never fight for attention — it carries it.
