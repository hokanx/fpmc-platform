2. **BTS moment** (15–30s) — real people, real work. Reveal phase.

4. **Talking moment** (15–45s) — soundbite from podcast/studio, with subtitles.
5. **Countdown card** (still) — date, merch, giveaway.
6. **Clip hook** (10–20s) — strongest moment from MV/podcast, hook in second 1.
