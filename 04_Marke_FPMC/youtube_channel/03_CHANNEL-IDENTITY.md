global gaming/EN, distinct from the FPMC platform audience. Add "A FPMC
production" to the About page and end cards; do not use FPMC navy/gold in
the channel UI (consistent with brand rules: logo & print tokens only).

## Visual identity

- **Style key (the channel look, locked across every episode):**
  cinematic painterly documentary illustration — muted palette of CRT
  phosphor teal, warm amber, deep charcoal; soft film grain; volumetric
  light; subtle scanline texture. Non-photoreal by design (also a workflow
  requirement). Full prompt in `episodes/EP01…` §Style key.
- **Avatar:** a minimal glyph — a play-button triangle fused with a rewind
  symbol, off-white on charcoal. Generate with `nano_banana_pro` (2 credits)
  or build free in Canva.
- **Banner (2560×1440, safe area 1546×423):** style-key artwork strip +
  wordmark + "Gaming's untold stories. Weekly." Generate at production time
  with the thumbnail batch (see `07_UPLOAD-PACKAGE-TEMPLATES.md`).
- **Thumbnail system:** one dramatic stylized scene + max 3 words in a heavy
  condensed face + one accent color per episode. Consistency = recognition.

## Voice & tone

- Narrator: calm, low-register, documentary gravitas (Attenborough-meets-
  noir). Voice is chosen ONCE from the Higgsfield voice picker at EP01
  production and the same `voice_id` is reused forever — voice consistency
  is channel identity.
- Writing: second person where possible ("You're a baker's apprentice…" →
  "You're a warehouse manager in New Mexico…"), concrete numbers, short
  sentences, no filler, never "in this video".

## Channel setup checklist (manual, free, ~30 min)

1. Create Google account for the channel (separate from personal).
2. youtube.com → create channel → set name + handle.
3. Upload avatar + banner; paste About text (incl. AI disclosure line).
4. Settings → Upload defaults: language EN, category *Education*,
   visibility *Scheduled*; enable "Altered content" disclosure default.
5. Verify phone (unlocks custom thumbnails + >15 min uploads).
6. Do NOT set channel-level "Made for Kids" — content is general audience.
7. Create playlists: "Gaming History", "Shorts".
8. Connect to VidIQ or TubeBuddy free tier (keyword/competitor data).
